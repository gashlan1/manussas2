## 19. FEE PROPOSAL SYSTEM - COMPLETE SPECIFICATION (Pages 24+)

### 7 Proposal Categories + Dual-Option Format

#### **Category 1: Standard Retainer**
- **Use Case:** Ongoing advisory with defined hours
- **Fee Structure:** Allotted hours + additional hours rate
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Threshold notifications at 75%/100%
- **Billing:** Monthly auto-invoicing with detailed breakdowns
- **Example:** "100 hours per month at SAR 500/hour, additional hours at SAR 600/hour"

#### **Category 2: Framework Retainer**
- **Use Case:** Ongoing advisory without defined hours
- **Fee Structure:** Hourly billing per rate schedule
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Monthly auto-invoicing with detailed breakdowns
- **Billing:** Automatic monthly invoice generation with hour breakdowns
- **Example:** "Hourly advisory at SAR 500/hour, billed monthly"

#### **Category 3: Fixed Fee**
- **Use Case:** Defined scope with phased deliverables
- **Fee Structure:** Fixed amount per phase
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Milestone-based billing
- **Billing:** Invoiced upon milestone completion
- **Example:** "Phase 1: SAR 50,000, Phase 2: SAR 75,000"

#### **Category 4: Contingency**
- **Use Case:** Outcome-dependent engagements
- **Fee Structure:** Success fee structure
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Outcome tracking
- **Billing:** Invoiced upon successful outcome
- **Example:** "20% of settlement amount if case won"

#### **Category 5: Hybrid**
- **Use Case:** Combined fixed and variable components
- **Fee Structure:** Fixed base + variable component
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Dual billing tracking
- **Billing:** Base invoiced monthly, variable upon milestone
- **Example:** "SAR 30,000 base + SAR 200/hour for hours over 100"

#### **Category 6: MISA Services** (Incorporation)
- **Use Case:** Foreign investor incorporation (platform intake)
- **Fee Structure:** Fixed scope, fixed fee, advance payment
- **Numbering:** FP-MISA-2026-XXX
- **Key Feature:** Auto-populated from platform; admin override option
- **Billing:** Advance payment required
- **Example:** "Complete MISA incorporation package: SAR 15,000"

#### **Category 7: Dual-Option Proposals** (INNOVATION)
- **Use Case:** Flexible engagement models for same matter
- **Fee Structure:** Combines any two categories into single document
- **Numbering:** FP-2026-XXX (with dual option notation)
- **Key Feature:** Client selects preferred option before execution
- **Billing:** Based on selected option
